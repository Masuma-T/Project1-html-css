About Us

At Jiffy Listing, we specialize in real estate photography tailored to the vibrant and competitive Edmonton market. We believe every property has a story—and our mission is to help that story shine through compelling visuals that leave a lasting impression.

To achieve this goal, we use high-quality equipment and techniques to capture the property from a variety of angles and lighting conditions. We also pay close attention to composition and framing to create visually appealing images.

By working together, we can create a set of images that accurately and effectively represent the property and make it stand out on the real estate market.

Exterior Photography

High-quality exterior photographs can help to showcase the property's curb appeal and give potential buyers a first impression of the property. These images also highlight unique features like architecture, landscaping, and outdoor spaces.

They also give buyers a sense of the property's location and surroundings—especially valuable for homes in attractive neighborhoods.

Interior Photography

High-quality interior photographs showcase the property’s layout, design, and finishes, helping buyers visualize key features and amenities.

This includes open-concept layouts, stylish kitchens, or spa-like bathrooms—all critical in a buyer’s decision-making process.

Matterport Virtual Tours

Matterport virtual tours allow potential buyers to explore properties remotely with an immersive 3D walkthrough experience.

These tours are convenient, time-saving, and offer buyers full control over how and when they explore, especially during times when in-person viewings are limited.

Sellers also benefit by reaching a broader audience and shortening the selling cycle.

Floor Plans

Detailed floor plans help buyers understand a property's flow and layout before stepping foot inside. This increases confidence in the listing and helps it stand out.

Including a floor plan is a proven way to increase listing effectiveness and attract more qualified buyers.
